A simple Mac app to automatically obtain Kerberos tokens on login. Depending on what your Kerberos infrastructure supports, this allows you to do the following securely and without a password:

  • Access your AFS shares
  • Log in to SSH
  • Log in to Kerberos-enabled websites

Features:

  • Stores your Kerberos password securely in the Mac Keychain so that it can automatically login for you.
  • Sets up SSH to use your Kerberos tokens.
  • Obtains AFS tokens automatically (requires OpenAFS to be installed and set up properly)
  • If Google Chrome is your default browser, automatically sets it up to enable Kerberos authentication (requires administrator password. See why. Note: Safari enables Kerberos authentication by default. For Firefox, see here). Additional steps may be required on the website itself to enable Kerberos authentication.
  • Automatically renews Kerberos tokens when they expire
  • If you have no network connectivity, automatically obtains tokens when you regain connectivity
